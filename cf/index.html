C Foundation - a 2 day course

This course is primarily aimed at professional engineers that have a few years of programming experience in C. The course teaches the current C Standard (C99). The course aims to give a solid foundation in C programming, but there is also a lot of interesting stuff for those who already consider themselves experts in C. Do you understand sequence points? What is a restrict pointer? Compile time asserts? While aiming at building up a solid C foundation from scratch, the course is also sprinkled with lots of goodies that you can pick up and chew on.

Prerequisite

The course assumes that students program C on a daily basis. The course assume that students have read through Kernighan & Ritchie and are familiar with latest edition of Harbison & Steele.
